Parse an SQS DeleteMessageResponse element.

This implementation parses SQS DeleteMessageResponse elements, as defined by http://queue.amazonaws.com/doc/2012-11-05/QueueService.wsdl

For example:

<DeleteMessageResponse>
<ResponseMetadata>
<RequestId>7a62c49f-347e-4fc4-9331-6e8e7a96aa73</RequestId>
</ResponseMetadata>
